MATH 2940 Discussion

This is the webpage for Elliot Cartee's sections of MATH 2940: Linear Algebra for Engineers at Cornell University in Fall 2019.

Every week, I will post copies of the worksheets used in section (with solutions included at the end of the document). I may also include outside material that I think is interesting and relevant to what we are doing in the course.

While you are not required to view anything on this webpage, I still think you may find it useful.
